As the Lead Electrical Engineer for the Boomer Rocket Team (BRT) at OU, I am responsible for the avionics systems and payload integration for our NASA Student Launch competition entry in Huntsville, Alabama.

**Key Responsibilities:**
* **Schematic Design:** Developed comprehensive avionics schematics using Lucidchart.
* **System Integration:** Managed the interface between flight computers, GPS telemetry, and the payload deployment sub-systems.
* **Technical Documentation:** Authored the electrical sections for the Preliminary and Critical Design Reviews (PDR/CDR).
